🔓 Hitting a login wall? Click here to add your session cookie
What is this? Some sites (like LinkedIn) won't show job listings unless you're logged in. Pasting your session cookie here lets the app read the page as you — your data is never stored.

How to get your LinkedIn cookie (30 seconds):
  1. Open linkedin.com in your browser and make sure you're logged in
  2. Press F12 → click the Application tab
  3. On the left expand Cookies → click https://www.linkedin.com
  4. Find the row named li_at — double-click its Value and copy it
  5. Paste it above as: li_at=PASTE_VALUE_HERE
How to get your Indeed cookie (30 seconds):
  1. Open indeed.com in your browser and make sure you're logged in
  2. Press F12 → click the Application tab
  3. On the left expand Cookies → click the Indeed URL
  4. Find the cookie named CTK — copy its Value
  5. Paste it above as: CTK=PASTE_VALUE_HERE
How to get your Glassdoor cookie (30 seconds):
  1. Open glassdoor.com in your browser and make sure you're logged in
  2. Press F12 → click the Application tab
  3. On the left expand Cookies → click the Glassdoor URL
  4. Find the cookie named GSESSIONID — copy its Value
  5. Paste it above as: GSESSIONID=PASTE_VALUE_HERE
How to get your session cookie (30 seconds):
  1. Open the job site in your browser and make sure you're logged in
  2. Press F12 → click the Application tab
  3. On the left expand Cookies → click the site name
  4. Find the main session cookie (usually named session, auth_token, or similar) — copy its Value
  5. Paste it above as: cookie_name=VALUE
On mobile? Do this step on a computer — browser DevTools aren't available on phones.
